      What To Expect
      Tesla is looking to hire an enthusiastic and experienced Analyst to support our business in EMEA. Business Analytics team is focused on building the reporting necessary to measure key performance indicators for Tesla’s sales, delivery and service teams, and increasing efficiency through data and analytics.

      You will be tasked to provide proactive analyses helping to drive business programs and informed decisions. The Data Analyst should act as an advisor, ultimately helping leadership to identify and overcome challenges thereby helping to enhance the productivity of Tesla’s sales, delivery and service organization. The Analyst must be able to clearly communicate insights to the management teams. You will have the opportunity to develop and continuously improve the way we make decision based on data.

      What You'll Do

    • Develop and implement detailed and accurate reports for the management team, providing visibility into performance against key business objective
    • Manipulate and interpret large datasets to deliver actionable insights to the management
    • Design intuitive and elegant visualizations to communicate key insights that drive business decisions
    • Improve the sales, delivery and service organization’s access to data via improved data generation and processing by partnering with our system analysts teams
    • Develop new tools to promote productivity and efficiency utilizing Tableau, SQL, Python and Tesla in-house reporting tools
    • Communicate effectively with the regional managers to identify and address areas for improvement
    • Build and maintain advanced models to measure, analyze, and forecast key metrics for our business in EMEA


      • What You'll Bring

    • Highly collaborative and team-oriented, while also able to work independently
    • Ability to deliver under tight deadlines and work on multiple tasks and projects at once
    • Experience with Tableau (or similar tooling like MS PowerBI, Qlik), SQL, and Microsoft Excel. Experience with Python and/or R is a plus
    • Excellent quantitative skills and a deep understanding of statistical methods with an ability to inspire confidence in data-driven decision making
    • Strong written and verbal communication skill
